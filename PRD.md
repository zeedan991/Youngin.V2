Module 1: AI Body Sizing & Fit

The Workflow: Users upload a front and side photo

The Output: The system extracts 33 distinct body landmarks (like shoulders, hips, and knees) and calculates the exact width and depth of the user's body. It provides highly accurate measurements in centimeters for the chest, waist, and hips, eliminating the need for a physical measuring tape.

Module 3: Global Brand Aggregator

The Workflow: A centralized shopping feed that displays millions of products from massive global brands like Nike, H&M, Zara, and ASOS.

The Business Model: When a user clicks to buy a brand's item, they are redirected to the brand's official website via an affiliate tracking link. If they buy it, your platform automatically earns a commission (ranging from 1% to 11% of the sale) without you ever needing to handle the inventory or shipping.

Module 4: Premium Subscription Thrift Shop

The Workflow: A recurring monthly subscription service. Users fill out a detailed style questionnaire regarding their favorite aesthetics, colors, and fits.

The Output: Using the user's AI body measurements, the platform curates a customized box of 5 high-quality, secondhand vintage items and ships it to them every month, creating a steady stream of recurring revenue.

Module 5: The Tailor Marketplace

The Workflow: A dedicated hub connecting users to local tailors. Users can browse tailor portfolios, view their pricing, and book digital or physical appointments.

Manufacturing: The user securely sends their custom 3D design and their AI measurement profile directly to the tailor to have the physical garment sewn.
